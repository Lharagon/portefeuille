About Me

Web Developer

Hello! My name is Luis H. Garcia and I live in Los Angeles, CA.

After working as a chemist for a couple of years, my passion for programming began after taking an online course implementing biology concepts using code. Today, I have proficient knowledge in Web and iOS developement, and I'm improving everyday. I love learning new technologies, solving problems, creating and improving projects, and sharing ideas.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy thinking about coding, riding my bicycle, exploring the city, going on hikes, admiring nature, learning human languages, and finding ways to give back to my community.
